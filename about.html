Firm story

From compliance execution to trusted advisory partner.

The firm's foundation is in audit, tax, and accounting, but the client need was always broader. Businesses required more than filings. Founders needed guidance around setup choices and banking readiness. Families needed clarity on residency, asset ownership, and succession. Over time, IRAA Global expanded into a more integrated advisory model because that is what the market actually required.

Today the practice serves as a strategic finance and structuring partner for clients who want a single trusted team that can move between statutory requirements and higher-order decisions without losing continuity.

For operating businesses

We support entrepreneurs and finance teams who need their reporting, tax, and governance to keep pace with growth.

For incoming founders

We help new entrants choose the right setup, prepare for banks and regulators, and avoid avoidable structural mistakes.

For private clients

We advise globally connected families where residency, Indian assets, UAE structures, and succession all sit on the same table.

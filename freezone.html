Are all free zones basically the same?

No. Authority fit, activity flexibility, visa rules, workspace terms, and banking outcomes can differ materially across free zones.

Can you help compare free zone and mainland options first?

Yes. IRAA helps clients compare the operating realities, not just the promotional summaries, before choosing the route.

Do you support the post-incorporation stage too?

Yes. The service can continue into banking preparation, bookkeeping readiness, tax support, and operating follow-through.

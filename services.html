Process
  1. 01

    Understand the facts

    We start by clarifying the business, ownership, deadlines, jurisdictional touchpoints, and the actual decision or compliance issue in front of you.

  2. 02

    Map the right service path

    We determine whether the matter sits within audit, tax, setup, accounting, corridor advisory, or private advisory, then define the scope clearly.

  3. 03

    Carry it through

    Where required, we take the work through registration, reporting, filings, documentation, and follow-through so the advice becomes operational.

Can IRAA support both company and shareholder-level issues?

Yes. That is one of the core advantages of the firm's model. Many assignments touch both the operating company and the individual decision-maker.

No. Some matters are one-off projects. Others are better served with ongoing monthly or quarterly support.

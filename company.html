Company Structure

How the IRAA brand is organized.

Iraa Global refers to the holding entity, Iraa Global LLC FZ, which owns and manages the IRAA brand and oversees its associated entities. Iraa Global LLC FZ does not directly provide services to clients.

IRAA brand network

Affiliated entities registered in Dubai Mainland.

The IRAA brand operates through a network of affiliated entities, all registered in Dubai Mainland, including IRAA Accountants LLC, IRAA Management Consultants LLC, IRAA Properties LLC, and Rays and Insight Chartered Accountants LLC, a partner firm.

Each entity is a separate legal entity and operates independently. The relevant contracting entity or partner firm depends on the service, scope, and engagement being undertaken.

Important note

Separate legal entities

The affiliated entities and partner firm operate independently. References to IRAA Global, IRAA, or the IRAA brand should not be read as creating a single legal partnership between all entities.

Engagement letters, invoices, regulatory references, and service responsibility should be reviewed by reference to the specific entity or partner firm named in the relevant client engagement.
